The Visualizations for this data set is as:

This graph on the left compares the total net revenue for the years 2015, 2016, 2017. We can say the total net_revenue decreased in the year 2016 and then increased in 2017.

This graph on left compares the total revenue for new and existing customers in the year 2016. We can say that the revenue collected from existing customer is approx 41% that of new customer.

This graph on left compares the total revenue for new and existing customers in the year 2017. We can say that the revenue collected from existing customer is approx 9% that of new customer. This ratio is pretty low as compared to previous year 2016.

The graph on the left depicts the mean revenue for each customer per year. Although the total revenue of 2016 was less than compared to the neighbouring year, the mean revenue for 2016 is higher.

We can see there is an overlap between some customers for all the years. If we were to check the revenue for these common customers in all the years, we can see the revenue from these existing customers is decreasing. The downgrade is not that steep for 2015 to 2016 but for 2016 to 2017 its a downfall.
